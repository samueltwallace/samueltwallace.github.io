Workstation and Machines

I am currently doing everything off of a laptop. It is a Dell Inspiron 15 5000 Series, it is 5 years old, and runs like a charm with some operating system tweaks. I have an HP monitor and a Ducky One Mini 2 Keyboard (though I will be building a new one soon). I dual boot Windows 10 and Void Linux (the musl version, on a whim). I also have a Kenting writing tablet when I teach.

I also have another machine that serves as a personal server I use on my wireless network for file storage and long-running processes. It is an old laptop of my sister's, a Toshiba Satellite. I currently run a personal archive of (semi-illegally) downloaded paper and textbook PDFs served over Nginx. I am planning on changing operating systems on this machine from the current Void Linux to FreeBSD (for reasons that I will share soon).
